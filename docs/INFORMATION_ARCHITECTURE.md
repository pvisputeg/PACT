# PACT information architecture

The portfolio is the enterprise control plane; an Outcome Room is the governed workspace for one measurable result. Operation Northstar is the end-to-end flagship, while five secondary outcomes demonstrate platform breadth as synthetic portfolio records.

```text
Mission Control
├── Northstar Plant 7 Digital Twin
├── Outcomes
│   └── Strategic Material Recovery
│       ├── Investigation
│       ├── Define Outcome
│       ├── Verify Signal
│       ├── Explore Impact
│       ├── Compare Strategies
│       ├── Independent Audit
│       ├── Human Authorization
│       ├── Coordinate Execution
│       ├── Measure Outcome
│       └── Closeout and Learning
├── Investigations
├── Approvals
├── Action Graph
├── Outcome Ledger
└── Replay
```

Every route reads the same persisted Northstar workflow state. The plant twin changes with tool-confirmed actions; the ledger and replay show the same evidence, authority, guard, action, observation, and learning chain.
